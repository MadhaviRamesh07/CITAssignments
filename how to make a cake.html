how to make a cake

  1. Ingredients:

    • ½ cup (113g) unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 cup (200g) sugar
    • 2 large eggs
    • 2 tsp vanilla extract
    • ½ cup (120ml) milk

  2. Preheat & Prep

    • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
    • Grease and flour your cake pan (or use parchment paper)

  3. Mix Dry Ingredients

    • In a bowl, whisk together: flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside

  4. Cream Butter & Sugar

    • In another bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy (about 2–3 mins)

  5. Add Eggs & Vanilla

    • Beat in eggs one at a time
    • Stir in the vanilla

  6. Combine Wet & Dry

    • Add the flour mixture in two parts, alternating with milk.
    • Example: flour → milk → flour → mix until smooth

  7. Pour & Bake

    • Pour the batter into the pan
    • Bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean

  8. Cool Down

    • Let it cool in the pan for 10 mins, then transfer to a wire rack

  9. Optional Frosting Idea

    • ½ cup butter + 1 ½ cups powdered sugar + 1 tsp vanilla + 1–2 tbsp milk → Beat until smooth and spread it on top once the cake is fully cool.
